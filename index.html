OUR MISSION

Empowering the youth to achieve their full potential by providing comprehensive mentoring programs that foster personal growth, academic success, and career development.

Read More

G1 ~ Inform and educate

provide students with the information and support they need to make informed decisions about their future

G2 ~ Competiveness

foster a culture of healthy competition and learning, and to encourage youth to develop their critical thinking and problem-solving skills

G3 ~ Training

provide hands-on training and support to individuals who are interested in developing new skills or enhancing their existing ones

G4 ~ Socialization

provide a safe and welcoming environment where individuals can connect with others, build relationships, and have fun

Services

Guidance

Our career guidance services are available to senior high schools, junior high schools, and lower and upper primary schools. We work with students to help them identify their strengths and interests, and to explore potential career paths that align with their goals

Organize Uplifting Competitions

We also organize quiz competitions for churches, primary schools, and junior high schools.

Developement

Our skill development program focuses on electrical/electronic technology, pastry (bakery), and computer literacy.

Sanitation

We are also committed to giving back to our community, and we organize cleanup exercises to help keep our environment clean and tidy

Physical Health

we provide health talks on topics such as HIV/AIDS, drug abuse, proper use of contraceptives.

Social Health

We address the raising concern of proper use of social media in our daily lives of individual and their mental health
